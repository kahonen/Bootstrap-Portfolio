About Me

My name is Kristin Ahonen, designer extrodinare! I've always had a passion for art, and technology. Up until a few years ago, I didn't realize these worlds could meld together so perfectly! I've dabbled in the world of HTML way back when MySpace was a thing, like back when it was THE thing. I then moved my career to the Graphic Design world and gratuated from Savannah College of Art and Design. I then finally put the two pieces of the puzzle together and realized I could use my love of Graphic Design and my interest in coding and turn the two into the ultimate career! So here I am embarking on a career change and hoping for the best! :)
